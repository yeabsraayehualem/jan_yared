Privacy Policy for EJAT App

Effective Date: November 10, 2025

EJAT App (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the EJAT mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Camera Access

The EJAT App requests access to your device’s camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes used for attendance tracking.

  • We do not take photos or record videos.
  • We do not store, transmit, or share any data from your camera.
  • All QR code scans are processed locally on your device.

2. Data Collection and Storage

The EJAT App does not collect, store, or share any personal data from your camera or QR code scans. All data processing occurs securely within the App.

3. Third-Party Access

The EJAT App does not use third-party analytics or advertising services that access camera or personal data.

4. User Consent

By using the EJAT App, you consent to the use of your device’s camera solely for QR code scanning as described in this Privacy Policy.

5. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available at the URL where this policy is hosted.
